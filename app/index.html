Harvest Fresh Story

Incredible Popularity.
All-natural Goodness.

We start with the finest hand-picked Hass avocados, then let them ripen to perfection. What you get is natural goodness and consistency every time with seamless recipe integration across the menu. From slices and dices to smooth and chunky, we have it all, all year long.

  • 100% Hass avocados provide rich, nutty flavor and creamy texture
  • All natural ingredients with no additives or preservatives for fresh-like flavor
  • Full line of offerings from guacamoles and pulp to cut fruit
