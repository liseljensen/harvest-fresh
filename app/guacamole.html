Guacamole

Our exceptional chefs have crafted four outstanding guacamole options using our hand-picked Hass avocados and authentic ingredients. Serve as is or add your own ingredients for a house-made signature guacamole. So simple. So good.

Western Guacamole

Chunky texture and homemade flavor seasoned with onion, red bell pepper and a touch of jalapeño.

Application Idea

Add Harvest Fresh™ Western Guacamole to a vegetarian wrap with white beans, red cabbage & cheddar. View full recipe.
